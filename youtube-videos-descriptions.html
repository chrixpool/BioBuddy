Youtube Videos Descriptions

Youtube Videos Descriptions play a major role in discoverability. The first 100 words are critical — they appear in search results and above the fold on watch pages. Use your main keyword in the opening sentences, then add timestamps, links, and a clear CTA to subscribe or visit your site.

SEO structure for descriptions

  1. Opening (first 2–3 sentences): primary keyword + short hook.
  2. Summary paragraph: explain what the video covers.
  3. Timestamps: list timecodes for sections (improves UX & watch time).
  4. Links & CTA: subscribe, related videos, resources.

Template (copy & adapt)

[Hook + keyword]
Short 1–2 sentence summary of what this video covers.

00:00 Intro
00:45 Tip 1
02:30 Tip 2

Subscribe: https://youtube.com/yourchannel
Resources: https://biobuddy.app/resources

Practical tips

  • Place the most important info within the first 100 words.
  • Use natural language and avoid keyword stuffing.
  • Include affiliate/disclosure notes if needed.
