how to add digital creator to facebook profile

how to add digital creator to facebook profile: adding the label "Digital Creator" makes your purpose and niche clear to visitors. Below are clear steps for both personal profiles and Facebook Pages so you can display “Digital Creator” where it matters.

For personal Facebook profile (About → Work)

  1. Open your Facebook profile and click About.
  2. Under Work and Education, click Add a workplace.
  3. In the Company or Position field type: Digital Creator.
  4. Select visibility and click Save.

For a Facebook Page

  1. Go to your Page → Click Edit Page Info.
  2. Under Categories, search for Digital Creator or choose the closest match (e.g., Creator, Artist).
  3. Update the About or Short Description to include “Digital Creator — [niche].”
